Constellation List

Orion
A prominent constellation, known for its bright stars, including Betelgeuse.
Visible in: Winter
Ursa Major
Known for the Big Dipper, a group of stars that form part of the larger Ursa Major constellation.
Visible in: All year round
Andromeda
A spiral galaxy, also the namesake of a famous constellation near the Pegasus constellation.
Visible in: Fall to Winter
